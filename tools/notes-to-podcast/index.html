Frequently Asked Questions About Notes to Podcast

Is this Notes to Podcast tool free?

Yes! Free plan gives you 30 credits per month (1 credit = 1 minute of generated audio). That's about 15 short podcasts or 3-4 full-length ones. No credit card required.

Will it work with messy notes?

Yes! The AI is designed to handle various note formats — bullet points, outlines, numbered lists, and prose. It organizes the content into a coherent conversation.

Can I use this for exam revision?

Definitely. Convert your revision notes into podcast format and listen repeatedly. The Deep Dive style works best for comprehensive exam preparation.

How is this different from just reading aloud?

Two hosts discussing your notes creates natural reinforcement — one host explains concepts while the other asks clarifying questions, similar to having a study partner.

Can I download the podcast?

Yes! You can download your generated podcast as an MP3 file. Pro and Ultra plans have high-quality audio with ElevenLabs voices for a more natural listening experience.

Can I use the generated audio commercially?

Yes, all generated audio on paid plans comes with full commercial rights. Free tier is for personal use only. Upgrade to Basic or Pro for commercial licensing.
