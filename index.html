Frequently Asked Questions

Is this like Google NotebookLM?

Yes! Similar to NotebookLM's Audio Overviews, but you can use any content — URLs, PDFs, or plain text. Sign up free for 30 credits/month to start generating.

How many podcasts can I generate for free?

The free plan gives you 30 credits per month (1 credit = 1 minute of generated audio). That's about 15 short podcasts or 3-4 full-length ones. Upgrade to Pro for 1,000 credits/month.

What languages are supported?

Currently English, Chinese, Spanish, Japanese, and Portuguese. More languages coming soon.

Can I download the podcast?

Yes! You can download your generated podcast as an MP3 file. Pro and Ultra plans have high-quality audio with ElevenLabs voices.

Can I use the audio commercially?

Yes, all generated audio on paid plans comes with full commercial rights. Free tier is for personal use only.
